Welcome to AIoT World Lab

Greetings, fellow innovators! AIoT World Lab is a student‑run research and development hub founded by passionate engineers from Hanoi University of Science and Technology. Our mission is to explore the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and the Internet of Things, transforming bold ideas into tangible solutions.

On this website you will find a curated collection of our academic projects, capstone designs, and open‑source prototypes—each accompanied by comprehensive documentation, schematics, and GitHub repositories. We believe in open knowledge: every line of code, every PCB layout, and every data set is shared so that the community can build upon and learn from our work.

Whether you are a fellow student, an industry partner, or simply an enthusiast eager to dive into the AIoT realm, we invite you to explore our portfolio, collaborate with us, and push the boundaries of innovation together.
